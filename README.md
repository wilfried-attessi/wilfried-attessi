## From networks to data — and loving every bit of it.

I started out configuring routers and monitoring network 
infrastructure in Abidjan, then founded and ran my own IT 
company for three years. Along the way I realized the part 
I loved most wasn't the hardware — it was reading the patterns 
in the numbers.

So I came to Canada to specialize. Now I analyze open data — 
health systems, housing markets, fintech transitions — and 
turn it into decisions that actually matter.
